Professional style report generator – Abander TagControl can generate reports in ASCII text and HTML formats. You may export Lyrics, Comments and Album information to:

• CSV (Comma separated)
• Playlist (*.m3u)
• HTML
• TXT (text files)

HTML:
The HTML report has many adjustments (the coding, font, colors, style, references). You may send the information on your entire music collection to your friends.

Playlist:
You may create playlists (*.m3u) for Media Players. Also in the Options screen on the general tab you may control the default naming context of the playlists when saving.

CSV:
This allows you to export the information on your musical collection to other programs. You may export Tag and MPEG information in textual comma delimited CSV a file. This format allows programs such as Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Access and others to access the report.
